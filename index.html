About Farina Swallow

Farina Swallow is a modern, safe, and quick alternative to traditional cassava swallow foods. It is made from 100% cassava extract, enriched with pure fibre, and processed to eliminate harmful compounds such as cyanide.

Benefits
  • Safe: processed to reduce cyanide
  • Added fibre for digestion & satiety
  • Quick & easy to prepare (3 minutes)
  • Gluten-free and natural
Preparation Methods
  1. Swallow: Boil water, stir in Farina until smooth
  2. Drink: Soak in water for a fibre-rich drink
  3. With Protein: Serve with meat, fish or chicken

Health & Nutrition Benefits

All our Farina products are made from cassava extract with added fibre, ensuring they are cyanide-free, additive-free, No preservatives added and NAFDAC-approved for safety. Proudly Nigerian and naturally wholesome, they support digestion, weight control, and blood sugar management. Based on nutritional profiles, our products offer high energy from carbohydrates, enhanced fibre for satiety, and additional nutrients from variants like almond and tiger nut.

  • Farina Swallow (Plain): Designed to fuel your day with essential nutrients that promote endurance and vitality., naturally gluten-free, and fibre-enriched for better digestion, slower sugar absorption, and weight management. Ideal for those seeking a simple, traditional swallow alternative.
  • Farina Almond Mix: Boosts protein, healthy fats, and micronutrients (like vitamin E and magnesium) for improved satiety and nutritive value. Studies show acceptable sensory qualities and increased mineral content, making it great for health-conscious consumers.
  • Farina Tiger Nut Mix: Rich in fibre for digestion, weight control, and blood sugar management, with a nutty flavor and gluten-free appeal. Offers low glycemic index benefits, aids cholesterol control, and provides healthy fats for overall wellness.
  • General Benefits: All variants are 100% organic, gluten-free, and help with digestion, weight watching, and sugar control. Compared to traditional cassava products, our fibre-enriched versions reduce glycemic load and enhance nutritional value without compromising taste.
